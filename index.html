LAC Tool

Download




An open source app, lets you do most of LAC related stuff such as importing maps and wallpapers.

Features
- Managing LAC maps
- Managing roles of LAC maps
- Managing LAC wallpapers
- Viewing LAC screenshots
- Android 11 support

Requirements
- At least Android 4.2 to run
- Android 4.4 or above recommended

Android 11 support
The app can manage LAC data even in Android 11.
